# /rewrite-check

Apply the WTFB 6-step rewriting methodology to your screenplay.

## Arguments
- `[screenplay-file]` - Optional: Path to screenplay file

## Workflow

### Step 1: Identify Script
```
Ready to apply the WTFB rewriting methodology!

This is a comprehensive process that will:
  1. Identify all story threads
  2. Chart arcs across acts
  3. Find holes in your structure
  4. Grade every scene
  5. Elevate weak scenes
  6. Compress for maximum impact

Which screenplay should we analyze?
```

### Step 2: Story Thread Identification
```markdown
## STEP 1: IDENTIFY THE STORIES

List every story thread by completing "The story of..."

### Main Plots
1. The story of [protagonist] who [journey]

3. The story of [theme exploration]

### Subplots
4. The story of [subplot A]
5. The story of [subplot B]

[Continue for all threads identified]
```
