# /character-interview

Conduct an in-depth character interview using the 80-question WTFB methodology.

## Arguments
- `[character-name]` - Required: Name of character to interview

## Workflow

### Step 1: Setup
```
Let's interview [CHARACTER NAME].

Grab a drink, get comfortable - we're going deep into
who this character really is.

I'll ask questions in groups. Answer as if you ARE the
character, or describe what they would say.

Ready to begin?
```

### Step 2: Background Section (Questions 1-10)
```
BACKGROUND

1. When and where were you born?
2. What's your favorite color?
3. What type of birth did you have?
4. What makes you laugh?
5. What makes you sad?
6. What makes you angry?
7. What makes you cry?
8. How do you make a living?
9. How do you feel about your mother?
10. How do you feel about your father?
```

### Step 3: Preferences Section (Questions 11-20)
```
PREFERENCES

11. What sports do you love to play?
12. What sports do you love to watch?
13. What's your favorite movie?
14. What's your favorite music?
15. Favorite TV show?
16. Favorite beverage?
17. Favorite food?
18. Political affiliation?
19. Do you gamble?
20. Do you have kids?
```

### Step 4: Relationships Section (Questions 21-30)
```
RELATIONSHIPS

21. Do you like kids?
22. Are you married?
23. Have you ever been married?
24. Do you believe in marriage?
25. What religion are you?
26. Most exciting thing that happened to you?
27. Most traumatic thing that happened to you?
28. What type of home do you live in?
29. How is it furnished?
30. Any physical diseases?
```

### Step 5: Psychology Section (Questions 31-40)
```
PSYCHOLOGY

31. Any physical disabilities?
32. Ever been in love? With whom? When?
33. Your first sexual encounter - where and when?
34. What one thing about the world would you change?
35. Your most recent dream?
36. Do you smoke or drink?
37. Do you use drugs?
38. What are three of your character flaws?
39. What's your goal in life?
40. Strongest physical attribute?
```

### Step 6: Self-Image Section (Questions 41-50)
```
SELF-IMAGE

41. What do you like most about your body?
42. What do you like least about yourself?
43. Who would you change places with?
44. Who do you idolize?
45. Who do you worship?
46. Are you aggressive?
47. Are you passive?
48. Do you read the newspaper?
49. Do you read books?
50. Do you read magazines?
```

### Step 7: Personality Section (Questions 51-60)
```
PERSONALITY

51. Favorite actor/actress?
52. What animal would you choose to be?
53. How do you dress?
54. How do you show or protect your feelings?
55. How do you feel about death?
56. How do you get along with other people?
57. How did your father influence you?
58. Your mother?
59. Your siblings?
60. Do you have brothers/sisters?
```

### Step 8: History Section (Questions 61-70)
```
HISTORY

61. Childhood strict or lenient?
62. Overprotected? Rejected? Loved?
63. Family's economic status?
64. Smart? Intelligent? Savvy? Slow?
65. High school? College?
66. Like school? Teachers? Classmates?
67. Do you travel? Where? Why?
68. Friends? Who? Why? How'd you meet?
69. Hobbies? Interests?
70. Daydream? Worry? Stress?
```

### Step 9: Worldview Section (Questions 71-80)
```
WORLDVIEW

71. Outlook on life - positive or negative?
72. How do you deal with hostility? Ridicule?
73. What's your health like?
74. What's your self-image?
75. How do you feel about your size? Looks?
76. Voice - shrill? High? Low?
77. Impulsive or self-controlled?
78. Energetic or sluggish?
79. Do you work out?
80. How do you feel about the opposite sex? Same sex?
```

### Step 10: Generate Dossier
Create character dossier document:

```markdown
# Character Dossier: [NAME]

## Vital Statistics
- Birth: [when/where]
- Occupation: [job]
- Residence: [home]

## Physical
- [key attributes]

## Psychological Profile
- Flaws: [three flaws]
- Goal: [life goal]
- Fears: [key fears]

## Background
- [key history points]

## Relationships
- [key relationships]

## Voice & Manner
- [speech/behavior notes]

## Story Function
- Role: [protagonist/antagonist/supporting]
- Arc: [how they change]

---
*Character Interview Complete*
```

## Success Criteria
- [ ] All 80 questions addressed
- [ ] Answers reveal character depth
- [ ] Contradictions/complexities noted
- [ ] Character dossier generated
- [ ] Voice and mannerisms clear
